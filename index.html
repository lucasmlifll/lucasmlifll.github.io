Rules for Forest Hill Centre

Main / General Rules

1. Use common sense.
2. Don't die of fall damage.
3. Please do not bring in your own food, drinks, or snacks. This is to prevent any allergies or poisoning from occuring. Instead, you can come to the caf√© on-site with plenty of options for you to enjoy!
4. Do not bring in any TNT or dynamite.
5. Don't do activities beyond your level, or anything that you know could be potentially dangerous. It may put you at risk of emotional (or physical) trauma.

Behavioural Rules

1. Please do not damage other people. Our visitors are very fragile and important to us.
2. Respect others and their property.
3. Follow instructions from staff.
4. Do not throw wet tissues onto the roof so that they stick there forever.
5. Refrain from climbing to any high spaces and jumping off with your body as a parachute.

Additional / Optional Rules

1. Do not enter the facility wearing any form of cosplay or costume that may be deemed inappropriate.
2. Don't wear shoes into the play centre.
3. Please refrain from entering the children's play centre if you're too old. Go visit one of our other fun attractions instead!
4. Most importantly, have fun!
